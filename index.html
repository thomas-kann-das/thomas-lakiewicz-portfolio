hi ⁠    thank you for visiting my page.

If you like to know, here is a little bit about me: i am a communication designer based near Hamburg.

I've been visualizing digital and print communication for more than 15 years now — and I still get excited every time a new project starts. I love good typography, clean layouts and excited by the unexpected.

I've worked in both classic advertising and on the corporate side, so I've seen brands from pretty much every angle. What I love most is helping companies find and shape their identity — turning ideas into something real and meaningful.

I'm a big believer in good teamwork and enjoy exploring new directions together with my clients (and even better, in interdisciplinary teams). I'm persistent when it comes to getting great results — and nothing makes me happier than seeing clients genuinely excited about the outcome.

  Don't be shy and come in contact, I'm looking forward to get to know you.
